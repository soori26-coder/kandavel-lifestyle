Privacy Policy

How Kandavel Lifestyle and Axenity Atelier OS collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: June 20, 2026

1. Introduction

Kandavel Lifestyle ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website kandavel.lifestyle and develops Axenity Atelier OS, a software platform used to manage digital content and social media integrations for our brand Margit Vintages and related business operations.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, contact us, or use Axenity Atelier OS and its integrations with third-party services including Pinterest.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, company name, and message content when you submit our contact form or email us directly.
  • Website Usage Data: Browser type, device information, pages visited, and referring URLs collected through standard web server logs.
  • Pinterest Account Data (via Axenity Atelier OS): When you authorize Axenity Atelier OS to connect with Pinterest, we may access information permitted by Pinterest's API and your granted OAuth scopes, including:
    • Pinterest account identifier and profile information
    • Boards, pins, and pin metadata
    • Analytics and performance data for pins and boards
    • OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to maintain the connection

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Responding to business inquiries and partnership requests
  • Operating and improving our website and services
  • Managing Margit Vintages brand content on Pinterest through Axenity Atelier OS
  • Creating, scheduling, publishing, and analyzing Pinterest pins and boards
  • Maintaining authorized API connections with Pinterest on behalf of authorized users
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our terms

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Axenity Atelier OS & Pinterest Integration

Axenity Atelier OS is developed and operated by Kandavel Lifestyle to manage social media content for our apparel brands. When integrated with Pinterest:

  • We access Pinterest data only through the official Pinterest API v5 using OAuth 2.0 authorization
  • Users must explicitly grant permission before any Pinterest data is accessed
  • API credentials (App ID and App Secret) are stored securely and are never shared publicly
  • We comply with the Pinterest Developer and API Terms of Service and Pinterest Developer Guidelines
  • We do not store Pinterest user passwords or session cookies
  • Pinterest data is used solely to provide the content management features of Axenity Atelier OS and is not used for unrelated purposes

5. Data Storage & Retention

Contact form submissions are processed via your email client and stored in our business email systems. Pinterest OAuth tokens are stored securely on our servers and are retained only as long as necessary to maintain the authorized connection or as required by applicable law. You may revoke Pinterest access at any time through your Pinterest account settings or by contacting us.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Pinterest: Data is transmitted to and from Pinterest's API as part of authorized integrations
  • Service Providers: Hosting providers (e.g., GitHub Pages) that process data on our behalf under confidentiality obligations
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Revoke Pinterest API authorization through your Pinterest account
  • Opt out of marketing communications
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at suresh.d@kandavel.lifestyle.

8. Cookies

Our website uses minimal cookies necessary for basic functionality. We do not use third-party advertising cookies or tracking pixels on this website. If we implement analytics in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.

9. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including secure storage of API credentials, HTTPS encryption on our website, and access controls for Axenity Atelier OS. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

10. Children's Privacy

Our website and Axenity Atelier OS are business tools not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Margit Vintages is a children's apparel brand; our products are marketed to parents and retailers, not directly to children.

11. International Users

Kandavel Lifestyle is based in India. If you access our services from outside India, your information may be transferred to and processed in India. By using our services, you consent to such transfer.

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our website or services after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
