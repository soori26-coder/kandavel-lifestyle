About Us

High-quality apparel manufacturing — built from the factory floor up with precision and transparency.

Kandavel Lifestyle is a high-quality apparel manufacturer specializing in T-shirts and essential fashion knitwear. Founded in 2020 in India, we engineer success for your brand with precision and transparency. Our vision is simple yet powerful: to be the most trusted and efficient knitwear manufacturing partner in the global apparel supply chain.

Our company was established through the collaboration of strategic leader Mr. D. Suresh (CCO and Co-Founder) and technical expert Mr. P. Gopal (CEO), combining two decades of manufacturing heritage with a forward-thinking, transparent business model.

In an industry often lacking transparency, we offer genuine, hands-on mastery. Both founders began their careers as basic trainees and meticulously worked their way up to key managerial roles, giving us a complete, ground-up understanding of the entire supply chain. Our unique strength is built upon the 40 years of combined, ground-up experience shared by our two co-founders.

Our Commitment to Responsible Manufacturing

We believe ethical and sustainable practices are fundamental to modern business.

  • Our People & Ethical Workplace We strictly follow all national labour laws, guaranteeing fair wages and reasonable working hours, and strictly prohibit child and forced labour. We maintain a safe, clean, well-lit, and respectful working environment.
  • Quality Materials & Environmental Intent We source raw materials from trusted, established suppliers. Our internal production processes are designed to reduce fabric wastage and optimize the use of water and energy.
  • Product Safety We select materials and dyes that meet common international safety standards. All primary material suppliers must provide REACH compliance, eco-friendly certifications (GOTS, OEKO-TEX, GRS), and guarantee non-toxic, safe garments for end-consumers.
  • Transparency & Accountability We welcome pre-production facility visits and third-party QC checks organized by our clients. We maintain clear records on the origin of primary materials, ensuring a traceable supply chain for every order.
